## Rho prior sensitivity

We use the prior of Scenario B in the methodology paper, `log ρ ~ N(log 0.2, 1)`. The posterior 95 % CI ([0.014, 0.019]) sits well below the prior's central mass, indicating that the data is strongly informative about ρ. A sensitivity analysis with `log ρ ~ N(log 0.05, 2)` produced an essentially identical posterior (mean shift −1.3 %, interval overlap 0.91), confirming that ρ is data-identified and not prior-pinned.

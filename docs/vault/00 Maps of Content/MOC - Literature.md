## How to use this folder

Each paper gets its own note in `40 Literature/`. Template:

```markdown
# Author Year — short title

## TL;DR
One paragraph.

## Why it matters here
- Connects to [[concept]] because ...
- Differs from this dissertation in [...]

## Key differences table
| | Their work | This dissertation |
|---|---|---|

## Citation
[BibTeX or formatted]
```

Don't try to fully summarize papers. Just capture **how each paper relates to your work** — that's what a literature note is for. The paper itself is on your shelf if you need details.
